Introduction
What is AssureQAi?
AssureQAi is a quality assurance platform built to audit 100% of your calls across inbound, outbound, collections, sales, and tele-verification. It delivers parameter-level defect detection, automatic TNIs (Training Needs Identification), and leadership-ready dashboards—all at market-challenging prices.
USP in One Line:
Full coverage at scale, deeper diagnostics, faster turnarounds—at a fraction of manual QA costs.

Product Details
Core Features & Specifications

End-to-End Call Auditing: 100% coverage or configurable sampling for all call types.
Configurable QA Forms: Parameters, weights, pass/fail logic, and fatal flags (e.g., DNC violations, missing disclosures).
Scoring & Status: Auto-calculated QA scores with GAR (Green/Amber/Red) distribution and pass/fail logic.
Defect Intelligence: Parameter-wise defect tagging across behavioral, process, compliance, system, and product knowledge.
TNIs & Coaching: Smart TNIs generated from defect patterns, linked to coaching plans, L&D modules, and closure tracking.
Dashboards & Reporting: Real-time drilldowns (agent → team → campaign → site), trend charts for QC Score, Defect Rate, Fatal Rate, and parameter compliance.
Ready Exports: One-click Excel (.xlsx) with pivots & conditional formatting, PDF summaries, and PPT packs for daily/weekly/MBR reviews.
Automation & Scale: Bulk ingestion of call dumps/recordings, API/SFTP integration, rule-based tagging by campaign/language/vertical.

Variants / Packages (Example positioning)

Essential: Core QA auditing + GAR scoring + defect tagging
Advanced: Adds TNIs, coaching linkage, and extended dashboards
